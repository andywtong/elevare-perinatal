Meet Our Registered Nurses

Dedicated to providing expert, compassionate care with a clinical foundation.

Li Lien

Registered Nurse (RN) | Founder, Elevare Perinatal

With over 18 years of experience in obstetrical nursing, LiLien brings a rare depth of clinical knowledge paired with a deeply compassionate, individualized approach to care. Her extensive background provides a grounded, confident foundation in medical assessment, evidence-based practice, and emergency preparedness — while her philosophy centers on respect, autonomy, and informed choice.

LiLien believes that pregnancy and birth should not be confined by the limitations of a strained healthcare system. Through years of bedside experience, she has witnessed how institutional constraints can unintentionally limit choice, continuity, and emotional support. This understanding fuels her passion to do better for women and families, offering care that prioritizes dignity, empowerment, and personalized decision-making.

At the heart of her practice is continuity of care, LiLien builds meaningful, trusting relationships with the families she supports. She believes that consistent presence fosters emotional safety, deeper connection, and greater confidence — allowing clients to feel truly seen, heard, and supported throughout their journey.
