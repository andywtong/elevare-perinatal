About Elevare

As Registered Nurse's with extensive obstetrical experience, we serve as a bridge between medical and physiological birth models. Our role is to facilitate informed decision-making, support your self-advocacy, and help create an empowering birth environment. Through emotional reassurance, physical comfort measures, and evidence-based nursing guidance, we work to reduce fear and promote a positive experience.

Every birth is unique. Our goal is to walk beside you with professional expertise and respect, supporting your vision and your journey into parenthood.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What qualifications do Elevare nurses have?
A: All Elevare nurses are Registered Nurses in good standing with the College of Registered Nurses of Alberta who are actively practicing in obstetrics in Calgary with a minimum of 10 years nursing experience. Our team holds advanced certifications including CPR, NRP, Emergency Midwifery Skills Workshop, and Fundamentals of Fetal Health Surveillance, insured, current criminal record check.

Q: When is the best time to apply?
A: Anytime during pregnancy or postpartum. Early booking allows us to build a trusting relationship and provide comprehensive, personalized support.

Q: How is RN-led support different from a traditional doula?
A: We are licensed Registered Nurses who combine clinical expertise with continuous emotional and physical labor support, offering enhanced safety, assessment, advocacy, and continuity of care.

Q: Who is this service best suited for?
A: Our services are ideal for clients seeking expert clinical support, calm advocacy, personalized education, and continuity of care — whether planning a hospital, birth centre, or home birth.

Q: Do you work with my healthcare provider?
A: Yes. We collaborate closely with your midwife, obstetrician, or family physician to support your birth plan while respecting medical guidance and hospital protocols.

Q: Do you attend home births?
A: Yes. We support home, birth centre, and hospital births, working alongside your chosen provider.

Q: What areas do you serve?
A: We serve Calgary and surrounding areas. Travel outside the city may be available upon request.

Q: Is your service covered by insurance?
A: Some plans offer partial coverage. Detailed receipts are provided for reimbursement. Please confirm directly with your insurer.

Q: How do I get started?
A: Contact us through our website to schedule a complimentary consultation.
