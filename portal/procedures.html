Standard Operating Procedures

Step-by-step guides for daily operations at Elevare.

Opening the Office

  • Check all lighting and climate controls.
  • Ensure the front desk is stocked with updated pamphlets.
  • Review the daily calendar for client appointments.

Closing the Office

  • Secure all physical file cabinets.
  • Log out of all shared workstations.
  • Activate the security system and lock the main entry.
